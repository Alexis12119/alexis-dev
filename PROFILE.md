## Visual Identity / Brand

For logo and branding generation:

- **Style:** Minimalist, editorial, structured, clean. No gradients, no blobs, no particle effects, no neon.
- **Colors:** Off-white background (#FAF9F6), near-black foreground (#111111), muted gray (#6B7280), light border (#E5E7EB).
- **Typography:** Geist for headings, Inter for body.
- **Tone:** Quietly confident. Professional. No "passionate developer" language.
- **Imagery:** Think monogram or wordmark. Simple geometric shapes. Thin lines. Generous whitespace. A mark that could sit comfortably in a print editorial or a clean UI.
- **Avoid:** Blue/purple gradients, floating blobs, particle effects, cyberpunk, glassmorphism, typewriter effects, skill percentages.
